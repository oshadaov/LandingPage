ABOUT

THE EDGE FITNESS established 7 years ago, first started providing fitness essentials to individuals with a passion for a healthier lifestyle. ‘THE EDGE FITNESS CLUB ’ was introduced in 2018, focusing on making sure clients have the perfect place to enhance their health. We at THE EDGE FITNESS CLUB take our customers’ suggestions and create an environment comfortable for everyone. We pride ourselves with the level of service we provide – all trainers are qualified fitness coaches whom specialize in all types of personal training varying from bodybuilding to Yoga. We provide our clients with customized meal plans to stay healthy at the gym, as well as home. The gym is a two storied premises alongside an outdoor rooftop with specialized functional fitness equipment and indoor air conditioned gym with a wide array of machines for every muscle along with a store for all your fitness essentials.
